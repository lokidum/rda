Rams Driving Academy - image drop folder
=========================================

The site shows a branded, styled placeholder for every photo slot until a real
image exists. Drop a file with the EXACT name below into this folder and the
placeholder disappears automatically (no code change needed), then rebuild.

Real photos, even good phone shots in nice light, are what push this site to
Apple tier. The placeholders are designed to look intentional until then.

Notes
-----
- JPG or WebP are both fine. Keep hero.jpg under about 400KB (use squoosh.app to compress).
- Use real photographs, not stock images.
- The hero image is also the Open Graph / social share image, so make it a strong one.
