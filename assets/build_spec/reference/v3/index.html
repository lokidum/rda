Good questions

Asked often.

Anything else on your mind? WhatsApp us. We genuinely do not mind quick questions.

I have an international licence. How many lessons will I need?+
It varies with your previous experience and the habits you have formed. Usually a few tailored lessons are enough to learn South Australian road rules, get comfortable on the left side of the road and tidy up habits before your practical test. Chat with us on WhatsApp and we will give you a personal estimate.
Do you offer automatic or manual lessons?+
We specialise in automatic driving lessons, which is the fastest and most popular way to get your P plates or convert your licence in Adelaide.
Can I use your car for my driving test?+
Yes. You can take the test in our fully insured, dual control academy vehicle, the exact same car you have been practising in, so nothing feels unfamiliar on the day. Ask about test day support when you book.
What is the difference between VORT and CBT&A?+
The VORT is a single one off practical driving test. The CBT&A is a competency based approach where you tick off tasks in a logbook across multiple lessons, with no big final test. We train and assess for both pathways and will help you pick the one that suits your nerves, timeline and budget.
How do I pay for lessons?+
We manage bookings directly through WhatsApp to keep things flexible, and accept bank transfer, PayID or cash on the day of your lesson. All payment details are shared when you confirm your booking.
Which suburbs do you cover?+
We run lessons across the wider Adelaide metro, including the CBD and the northern and north eastern suburbs. Message us with your suburb and we will confirm pick up arrangements.
I get nervous behind the wheel. Is that okay?+
More than okay, it is common. Lessons are structured at exactly the pace you need, in a calm and judgement free car, and the dual controls mean your instructor can step in at any moment. Plenty of our most anxious students are now everyday drivers.
How far in advance should I book?+
A few days ahead is usually enough for regular lessons. For test day car hire, message us as soon as you have your test date so we can lock the car in for you.
